The Eclipse Foundation is a community of 100M+

The Eclipse Foundation stands as a cornerstone of open-source collaboration, fostering innovation across a global network of developers and organizations. Known for its robust ecosystem and support for diverse projects, it continues to shape the future of software development through community-driven initiatives and cutting-edge technologies.
